Ally's Favourite Movies

The Top 5

1. The Dark Knight

Phenomenal film with a fantastic performance by Heath ledger.

2. Seven

The best crime film I've seen.

3. City of God

Fantastic and brutal film about growing up in the slums of Rio.

4. The Godfather

The greatest gangster/mafia movie ever made.

5. The Fighter

Potentially the best sport movie and a fantastic performance by Cristian Bale.
